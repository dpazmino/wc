## Caveats
- Cumulative props ('reach round X', 'eliminated by round X') are cleaner than 'exact stage' — the modal exit for most strong teams is R32 (the first cull), so exact-stage bets bunch up.
- Knockout ties are coin-flips and ~11 backfilled squads carry estimated Elo, so deep-run (SF/Final/Champ) cells understate true uncertainty.
